Mandelbrot visualization -- divergence and stability


Keybindings:


? display this help message
up, left, down, right pan
- and = zoom
p (P) switch to single (double) precision (slow, experimental)
, and . change color cycle speed
{ and } change exponent
c cycle color scheme

Color channel controls:

e and E change red minimum
t and T change red maximum
r and R change red phase

f and F change green minimum
h and H change green maximum
g and G change green phase

v and V change blue minimum
n and N change blue maximum
b and B change blue phase
